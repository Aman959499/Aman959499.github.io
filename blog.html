10 Ways to Reduce Plastic Pollution in Your Daily Life

Plastic pollution is one of the biggest threats to marine life. Here are some easy steps you can take:

  1. Use reusable water bottles instead of single-use plastic bottles.
  2. Bring your own shopping bags when going to the store.
  3. Avoid plastic straws; use metal or bamboo alternatives.
  4. Recycle properly according to your local guidelines.
  5. Support products with minimal plastic packaging.
  6. Participate in local beach and river cleanups.
  7. Educate friends and family about the impact of plastic pollution.
  8. Use refillable containers for personal care products.
  9. Compost organic waste instead of using plastic bags.
  10. Reduce online shopping packages with excessive plastic wrapping.

Our Latest Beach Cleanup Event and Results

We held a beach cleanup at English Bay, Vancouver, last weekend. Over 50 volunteers collected more than 200 kg of trash, including plastic bottles, straws, and food wrappers. Events like these not only clean our beaches but also raise awareness about the importance of ocean conservation.

We are planning monthly cleanups and workshops to educate communities about reducing single-use plastics. Join us next time and be part of the change!
